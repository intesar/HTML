The Real Cost of Unstructured Development Processes for Startups
Complete this Test and Find Out How Much
Money You’re Wasting
Background
Planning out a structured process for software development that reduces bugs and ensures better manageability in production may not be the top priority for early stage startups.

The only thing driving such startups is getting quick market validation. As a result, developers will take the easiest route to get there without worrying about Issues like software quality, scalability, continuous delivery, and application management in production.

As soon startups move from market validation to the growth stage, then an unstructured software development process may end up being very costly and in some cases, catastrophic. Onboarding new developers becomes a pain, the software quality suffers, and a lot of time is wasted in setting up environments.

In this paper, you will get a chance to understand the best practices for the different stages (or phases) of application development. Get ready for the test and see where your startup stands.
